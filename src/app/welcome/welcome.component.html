Life in Dublin: Three Greek Girls, One App, and a Furry Friend

Meet Danai, Efi, and Chrysa, three friends from Greece who share a house in Dublin. Chrysa is a software engineer, Efi is an HR, and Danai is a Biologist. They all work remotely and share their house with their beloved cat, Holly.
Living together, the girls quickly realized that keeping track of chores and tasks can be challenging. So, Chrysa decided to build an app that would make their lives easier. The app allows them to assign tasks to each other, track progress, and receive reminders.
When they're not working, Efi, Danai, and Chrysa love to explore Dublin, try new restaurants, and attend cultural events. They also enjoy spending time together in their cozy living room, watching movies, and playing board games.
Their cat Holly is the perfect companion, often curling up on their laps while they work or relaxing with them on the couch.
